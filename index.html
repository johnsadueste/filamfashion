Our Philosophy

Made from
memory,
worn today.

LIKHA — the Tagalog word for to create — was born from the tension of existing between two cultures and finding beauty in that in-between space. FilAm Fashion is where that story lives.

Every piece begins with Filipino textile heritage and is shaped by the American experience. Custom-made. Never mass-produced.

01

Heritage Textiles

We source authentic piña, abaca, and hablon weaves directly from Filipino artisan cooperatives.

02

Custom Made

Every garment is made to order. Your measurements, your story, your piece — nothing sits in a warehouse.

03

AI-Assisted Design

We use AI to generate editorial concepts, colorways, and style narratives — then human hands bring each one to life.
